In this section I will talk about hobbies and interests. My favourite hobby is linked with my work and that is travelling! I will list in order main reasons why i love travelling:

  1. One of my favourite reasons for having travel as a hobby is being able to immerse myself in different countries and culture. Even spending a week in another country gives me more understanding and appreciation of a culture than never having visited. When I can talk to people, live alongside them, shop in the places they do and feel out of my comfort zone in their world, it begins to help me realise some of what is important.
  2. I agree that the only way to truly comprehend the amazing places on Earth is to see and feel them with your own eyes. In that you get a completely different understanding of something and you can make your own concept of what it is and how it is really.
  3. Whilst travelling it is possible to meet new people and make friends aswell and my phone book has the numbers of a few that have become more than just people to me. Whilst most don’t become lifelong friends, the colourful characters we meet on the road become part of the stories we tell for a lifetime. To all of you, and the hundreds of others i have met, thank you.
